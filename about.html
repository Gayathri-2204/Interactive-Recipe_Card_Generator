Essential Cooking Tips & Techniques

A curated collection of pro-tips and fundamental guides to elevate your everyday cooking.

Beyond the Recipe

Great cooking isn't just following steps; it's understanding **technique**. These guides focus on fundamental skills—from knife control to perfect seasoning—that apply to any dish you create.

The Pinch Grip

A proper knife grip is the foundation of kitchen safety and efficiency. Using the **pinch grip** gives you maximum control.

Season in Layers

Don't wait until the end to add salt. **Seasoning at every stage** of cooking builds depth and complexity in flavor.

The Importance of Rest

**Resting meat** after cooking is the single most effective way to ensure a juicy, tender result. It allows the muscle fibers to relax.

Salting Pasta Water

The water you boil pasta in should be as **salty as the sea**. This is the only chance you have to season the pasta itself, not just the sauce.

Hot Pan, Cold Oil

Start with a **hot, empty pan**, then add your oil or fat. This prevents sticking and ensures a beautiful, even sear every time.

The Freshness Test

If you're unsure how fresh an egg is, use the **float test**. This technique is simple and essential before poaching or frying.
